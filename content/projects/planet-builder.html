PlanetBuilder is a Spherical Voxel Engine, and my very-first Unity3D side project.

This Unity pluggin generates planets made of cube. Most interesting feature is in the fact the cubes are not drawn in cartesian coordinates (like Minecraft) but on a surface looking like an inflated cube : walking on the sphere surface, cubes always appear horizontal.

Project also features some 3D noise generation methods, extracting a 3D heightmap with holes and caves from a 4D computational space.

A Web based version of this project, using BabylonJS engine, was later implemented.
