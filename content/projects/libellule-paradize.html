Theme of Ludum Dare 34 was Two Button Controls, my entry is a dragonfly flight sim with two buttons.

Using only A and D, a pretty dragonfly can float around a flower field and catch coins.

While not very easy to control, this strange aircraft has a pretty wide range of movement in a three dimensional space.
